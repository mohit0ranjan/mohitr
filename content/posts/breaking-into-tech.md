Being a student in tech right now is overwhelming. AI is coding better than us, the market is tough, and everyone has a "perfect" resume.

## Focus on Building

Don't just watch tutorials. Build things that break.

1. **Clone an app**, then add a feature it doesn't have.
2. **Build a tool** that solves a problem you have.
3. **Contribute to Open Source**. It's scary but worth it.

## The Cold Email

Most of my opportunities didn't come from applying on portals. They came from reaching out to founders and engineers directly.

> "Hi [Name], I saw you're building X. I built a small demo using your API and found a bug. Here is the fix."

This works 10x better than a generic cover letter.
